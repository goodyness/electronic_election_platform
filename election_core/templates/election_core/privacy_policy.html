Privacy Policy

1. Data Minimization

We only collect the minimum amount of data required to verify a voter's identity and ensure one-person-one-vote. Most voter data is hashed and salted at the point of ingestion.

2. Voter Anonymity

FlashVote is designed so that even we, as the platform providers, cannot link a specific ballot to a specific individual once it is cast. Our "blind ballot" protocol ensures absolute secrecy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade voter or organizer data with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for the maintenance of the platform and the execution of your elections.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR and international standards, you have the right to access your data and request its deletion. Please note that once a vote is cast and the results are sealed, the mathematical proof of that vote is permanent for integrity reasons, but it remains anonymous.
