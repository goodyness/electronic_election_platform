Privacy & Data Governance

NDPC Licensed Data Controller

01 Regulatory Compliance

FlashVote is fully licensed and regulated by the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC). We adhere strictly to the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and international best practices for data sovereignty and security.

02 Information We Collect

Institutional Data

We collect university-provided voter lists (Emails) uploaded by authorized Election Organizers for the sole purpose of voter accreditation.

Technical Metadata

IP addresses, device information, and browser types are logged for security auditing and to prevent fraudulent voting activities.

03 Data Minimization & Security

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data in transit and at rest is protected by AES-256 standard encryption.
  • Voter Anonymity: Once a vote is cast, the link between the voter's identity and their specific ballot is cryptographically severed.
  • Data Access: Only authorized system administrators and specific election organizers have access to voter lists.

04 Your Rights

Under the NDPA, users have the right to access their personal data, request corrections, and object to processing. Institutional users (Voters) should primarily contact their University Election Committee as the primary Data Controller, with FlashVote acting as the Data Processor.
