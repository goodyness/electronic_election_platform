The Architecture

Engineered for Trust

Six layers of security, transparency, and speed power every election on the FlashVote ledger.

Immutable Integrity

Every ballot is encrypted at the moment of submission. Our real-time ledger employs SHA-256 hashing to generate a deterministic audit hash, ensuring every vote is authentic, tamper-proof, and publicly verifiable.

SHA-256 Verified

Instant Deploy

Launch institution-wide elections in minutes. From candidate setup to voter accreditation, it's seamless and zero-config.

OTP Accreditation

Voters authenticate via a cryptographic 6-digit code sent to their verified email. Two-factor trust at every booth.

Visual War Room

Experience real-time transparency. Monitor election progress with our live tactical dashboard, featuring turnout heatmaps, vote-speed analytics, and a predictive final turnout indicator — all updating automatically.

Live Feed Auto-Refresh

Ballot Privacy

Our "Severance" protocol ensures we track that you HAVE voted, but never HOW you voted.

Audit Hash

After election closure, a deterministic SHA-256 hash is published publicly. Anyone can verify that results haven't been altered.
