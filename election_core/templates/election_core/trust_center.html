Trust Center

Security isn't an afterthought; it's the core of FlashVote. Explore the protocols that ensure every vote counts.

Cryptographic Secrecy

We use military-grade hashing and encryption to ensure that individual ballots can never be traced back to the voter. Our double-blind receipt system allows you to verify your vote exists without revealing your choice.

Immutable Audit Logs

Every system event—from election creation to result sealing—is recorded in a permanent, read-only audit log. This provides a transparent paper trail that impossible to manipulate or delete.

Real-time Verifiability

Our War Room allows admins to monitor voting pulse in real-time, ensuring zero downtime and immediate detection of any infrastructure anomalies.

Zero-Knowledge Focus

We aim to minimize data footprint. FlashVote doesn't need to know who you are to know that you are authorized to vote.
