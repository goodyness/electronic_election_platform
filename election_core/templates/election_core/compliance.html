Compliance & Standards

FlashVote is built to meet the most rigorous institutional requirements for integrity and data handling.

01 Data Protection (GDPR)

We adhere strictly to General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards. We do not sell voter data, and all personal identifiers are either encrypted or hashed to ensure voter anonymity throughout the process.

  • Right to be forgotten (Requestable via organization admin)
  • Minimizing data collection to necessary identifiers only
  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit (AES-256)

02 Institutional Integrity

FlashVote protocols are designed to prevent double voting and unauthorized access. Each ballot is cryptographically signed and stored in a temper-proof audit log.

03 Auditability

Every election concludes with a verifiable audit report. This report allows institutional observers to verify the mathematical validity of the results without compromising individual secrecy.
